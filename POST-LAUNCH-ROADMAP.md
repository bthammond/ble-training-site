# BLE Training — Post-Launch Roadmap

- [ ] Write and publish 3 blog articles (drafts are staged on /blog)
- [ ] Add 5 more blog articles targeting SEO keywords

### Lead Generation
- [ ] Connect Mailchimp to newsletter signup form (replace mailto)

- [ ] Get 3-5 real photos of testing centers (iPhone is fine)
- [ ] Add Google Reviews widget to footer or about page
